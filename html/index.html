Helix Preset Viewer

A free, open-source visualizer for Line 6 Helix / HX Stomp / HX Effects preset files. Upload a .hlx file to explore the signal chain, block parameters, snapshots, and footswitch assignments. Runs entirely in your browser — no files are uploaded to any server.
